Booking

Book your bike in for service.

Use the form below to send through your booking request. Bookings will be confirmed manually, so someone will be in touch after your request is received.

How it works

Simple booking process

  1. Fill in your contact details and let us know what service you think you need.
  2. If you are unsure, choose the Unsure option and add a few notes.
  3. Your booking request will be reviewed manually.
  4. You will be contacted to confirm the booking details.

Bookings will be confirmed manually.
