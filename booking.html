Booking

Book your bike in for service.

Personal service with fast turnaround - often same or next day. Call 021 0290 1656 for fastest service, or book online.

How it works

Simple booking process

  1. Click Book now to open our secure online booking page.
  2. Choose the service you need. Select “Other” if you’re unsure or just need one thing done.
  3. Choose the day you’d like to book. The system requires you to select a specific time, but your booking is for the full day. We generally ask that bikes are dropped off in the morning (or before).
  4. Your bike will be ready by the end of the day unless we let you know otherwise.

Online booking
