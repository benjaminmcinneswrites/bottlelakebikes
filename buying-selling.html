Buy a refurbished bike

Here at Bottlelake bikes we sell good quality, fully serviced bikes. This allows our customers to have full confidence in their purchase while getting much better value for money than buying new.

Sell us your bike

Looking to get rid of your bike with the least hassle possible? Have a chat to us, we can buy it from you!

This is a good option if you want a simpler process and would rather know your price upfront.

Let us sell your bike for you

Alternative to selling us your bike, we can sell it for you. To help you get the best price possible we will service, clean and polish the bike prior to sale.

This option is better suited to owners who want to aim for a stronger sale result and are comfortable with the bike being sold on commission.

Commission rates

Clear commission structure for bikes sold on your behalf.

Commission rates for bike sales
Sale range Commission
Sales up to $600 35% commission
Sales between $600 and $1500 30% commission
Sales between $1500 and $3000 25% commission
Sales over $3000 20% commission
Minimum commission $100

Note: We will provide an estimated sale price before listing the bike. If you want to know exactly how much you are getting and want the money instantly, you can sell us your bike instead.
