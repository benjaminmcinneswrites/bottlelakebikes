Service packages

Choose the level of service that suits your bike.

Fast, honest bike servicing with clear pricing and no surprises. We service all bikes including mountain, road, gravel, commuter, and e-bikes.

Not sure what you need? Book in and we’ll assess your bike on drop-off and recommend the right service.

General Service

$79

A proper mechanical check and tune to keep your bike safe, smooth, and reliable.

  • Brake tuning and adjustment
  • Gear tuning for smooth shifting
  • Safety & bolt check
  • Clean bike & wheels
  • Adjust hubs & headset
  • Inflate tyres
  • Chain lubrication

Full Service

$159

A complete mechanical refresh to restore smooth performance and reliable riding.

Includes everything in General Service, plus a full service of key mechanical systems:

  • Cable lubrication or replacement (new parts extra)
  • Wheel truing & spoke tensioning
  • Hub service
  • Headset service
  • Bottom bracket service

Drivetrain deep clean add-on

$39

Add this to any service to make your drivetrain run smoother and last longer.

  • Full removal of chain, cassette, and chainrings
  • Deep clean of all drivetrain components
  • Reassembly and lubrication
  • Replacement drivetrain parts fitted if required (parts extra)

Service add-ons

Extra work for bikes that need more specific attention.

The following add-ons are available to suit your bike’s specific needs. We don’t include them in standard packages, so you only pay for what your bike actually requires.

Available add-ons

  • Fork lowers - $65 per unit (plus seals if required)
  • Rear shock air can service - $65
  • Full suspension pivot clean & grease - $90 (bearing replacement available if required)
  • Dropper post service - $60 (plus parts if required)
  • Brake bleed - $25 (Shimano/Tektro), $40 (SRAM) (per brake)
  • Tyre sealant refreshed - $8 per wheel
