Service add-ons

Extra work for bikes that need more specific attention.

The following add-ons are available to meet the specific service requirements of your bike. We don’t include them in the packages because we don’t want to charge you for work that your bike doesn’t need.

Available add-ons

  • Fork Lowers — $60 per unit (plus seals if required)
  • Full Suspension Pivot Clean and Grease — $90 (bearing replacement available if necessary)
  • Dropper Post Service — $60 (plus parts if required)
  • Brake Bleed — $35 for Shimano + Tektro, $45 for Sram (price per brake)
